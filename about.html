ABOUT SPACE CATS

PURPOSE

“Save the Space Cats!” is a website designed for elementary students ages eight to twelve about basic computer science. Specifically it teaches kids four lessons that include- computer parts and their names, types of computer parts, programming languages and how to write a line of code. Computer Science is a growing field and we need to give the next generation the best tools to succeed.
